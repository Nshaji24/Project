Welcome!

This website will teach you the ins and outs of building your personal computer. Wheter you want to game or do other types of work on it, building your own computer has alot of benefits over buying a pre-built. The "Build a PC" menu option will let you jump right into the process!

Why build my own PC?

There are several benefits of building your own PC , some of the most important include:

  • More affordable When building your own PC you chose what parts go into it , and what parts fit your budget, you have alot o f room to work with in terms of picking your own parts and choosing what you want to pay for each part. You dont waste money on parts you dont need, for example, a Pre-Built might have a 750w power supply when you can save money and get a 600w instead.
  • You know whats in your PC.This one is huge. Some companies who sell Pre-Build PCs use cheaper hardware so they can save money. When building your own PC you know exactly what youre putting into it.
  • Troubleshooting is easier Since you built it yourself, you know your way around the PC and any hardware issues that occur you can fix with just a few searches or by just looking at your PC and checking your parts.
  • Maintance and Upgrades Building a PC yourself allows you to upgrade your parts whenever you want, so this way you dont have to keep buying a new computer/ gaming console every couple of years, you can just save the money and upgrade parts that may be outdated
